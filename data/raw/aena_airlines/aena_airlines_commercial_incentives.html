Additional discounts

CONNECTING PASSENGER FEES DISCOUNT

Passenger and security tax

40% off the passenger and security tax for passengers with a connecting flight: passengers with a connecting flight are those that, within a period of 12 hours, travel on two or more subsequent flights with a single ticket number to a different airport than the airport of origin.

SEASONALITY DISCOUNT

Passenger and security tax

20% off the passenger and security tax from 1 November to 31 March: This is currently applicable to seasonal airports in the Balearic islands (PMI, IBZ and MAH) as per Act 21/2003 and the seasonality conditions set.

OFF-PEAK DAYS

Passenger and landing tax
(Airports in the Canary Islands)

50% off the passenger and landing tax: When operating at airports in the Canary Islands during off-peak days
