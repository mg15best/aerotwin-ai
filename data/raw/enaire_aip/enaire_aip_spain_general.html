AIP

The aeronautical Information Publication (AIP) is the basic aeronautical information manual.

Contains information of a permanent nature as well as temporary information of long duration, and its use is essential for air navigation and airport operations. It is divided into three parts: General (GEN), En-Route (ENR) y Aerodrome (AD).

The information it contains is updated regularly by means of an amendment service and supplements to the AIP. To obtain the latest alerts do not forget to check the NOTAMs in force.
