    New developments at the airport

    VIP LOUNGE OPENING

    Gran Vía VIP Lounge

    Located on floor 0 of Terminal T4S, next to Gates S127 and S128.

    Aena Kids

    Summer workshops

    Adolfo Suárez Madrid-Barajas Airport will be holding summer workshops for children between 1 July and 31 August inclusive. The activities will take place in the children's area next to gate C46 (nursery in T2), from 8:00 to 20:00; the children's area next to gate K82 in T4, from 11:00 to 19:00; and in the children's area next to gate S11 in T4S, from 11:00 to 19:00.

    NEW ROUTE

    Madrid to Trípoli (Libia)

    With Medsky Airways. The new route will have two weekly frequencies: Tuesdays and Fridays.

    Weapons Inspection

    Office T1 Closure

    The office in Terminal T1 has been closed since 1 April. All activity will be handled at the Terminal T4 office until further notice. Any queries or issues arising during the closure (T1), will be dealt with by calling (+34) 917 466 410.

    RECOGNITION

    Best Airport in Europe

    Airports Council International (ACI) has awarded the Airport with the Best Airport in Europe award in the category of of over 40 million passengers.
